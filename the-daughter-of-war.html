The Daughter of War

2026 Directed by XPDevs Studio

Synopsis

Her father is killed. She joins the army under a false name. Her plan is simple: find the man who did it and make him pay. But the deeper she goes, the more she realises revenge costs more than she thought.

Set in the late 1700s. A story about identity, sacrifice, and what happens when the fighting stops and you have to live with what you did.

Characters

Elena Vasquez

Protagonist

A young woman from a small border town. After her father is killed in a raid led by a rival officer, she enlists in the army under the name Elias Vega. She is determined, resourceful, and driven by grief she does not fully understand.

Captain Aldric Kane

Antagonist

The officer responsible for the raid on Elena's village. A decorated soldier with a pragmatic view of war. He does not remember Elena's father, which makes his indifference more painful than his cruelty.

Sergeant Mara Holt

Supporting

A seasoned soldier who takes the new recruit under her wing. She sees potential in Elias and becomes both a mentor and an unknowing witness to Elena's double life.

Father Tomas

Supporting

A priest who knew Elena's father. He provides a moral counterweight to Elena's quest for vengeance, urging her to consider what she will become if she succeeds.

From the Writer

I have been sitting on this story for a while. A woman who refuses to accept the role the world gave her. Who decides to meet violence with violence, on her own terms. I wanted to look at what happens when justice becomes personal. When the line between right and revenge disappears.

The late 1700s worked because it was a rigid time. A woman dressing as a soldier was not just dangerous. It was a rejection of everything she was supposed to be. That tension is the whole film.
