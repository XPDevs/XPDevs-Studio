Short Film

The Last Light

A lantern sits on an old wooden table. A moth circles the flame. Two minutes.

2026 Directed by James Turner

Synopsis

A lantern on a table in a dark room. A moth circling the flame. Two minutes of light and shadow and a moth that will not stop.

The Last Light was made with code. No dialogue. No plot. Just a lantern, a moth, and the dark.

Details

Director James Turner
Year 2026
Runtime 2 Minutes
Genre Experimental
Format Generative Animation
Status Released

About This Film

We wanted to see if two minutes of code could feel like a film. The scene is simple. A lantern. A table. A moth. The light moves. The shadows move. Thats it.

We use AI to build the code. But the idea, the composition, the timing. That was a person.

XPDevs Studio is an XPDevs company. We use AI to build the code behind our films. Every story is written by a person.
